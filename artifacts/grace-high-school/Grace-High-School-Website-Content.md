## OUR PROGRAMMES (Academics)

**Heading:** Our Programmes
A comprehensive National Curriculum across O-Level and A-Level, complemented by vocational programmes that prepare students for life beyond school.

**Ordinary Level (S1 – S4)**
Four years of broad foundational education leading to the Uganda Certificate of Education (UCE), setting students up for A-Level and beyond.
*Subjects:* Mathematics, Sciences, English, History, Geography, CRE, SST, Languages

**Advanced Level (S5 – S6)**
Two-year advanced programme leading to the Uganda Advanced Certificate of Education (UACE), preparing students for university entrance.
*Subjects:* Sciences, Arts, Business, ICT, Agriculture, Economics

**Vocational Skills (Extracurricular)**
Practical skills training alongside academics, equipping students with capabilities that create real-world opportunities.
*Subjects:* Entrepreneurship, Life Skills, Sports, Arts & Crafts, Leadership, Welding

## ADMISSIONS

**Heading:** Join the Grace Family
Admissions are currently open for all classes — S1 through S6. We welcome students and families who share our commitment to faith, excellence, and vision.

### How to Apply
1. **Contact the School** — Call or email us to express interest and get an admissions form.
2. **Visit Our Campus** — Schedule a tour of our 28-acre campus in Gayaza, Wakiso District.
3. **Submit Application** — Complete the form with academic records and personal information.
4. **Placement Assessment** — Students may sit a brief assessment to identify the right class.
5. **Confirm Enrollment** — Receive your admission letter, pay fees, and report on opening day.

### Entry Points
- **O-Level — Senior 1 to Senior 4:** Direct entry into S1 for candidates who have sat PLE. Transfers into S2–S4 are considered subject to vacancies and a short placement assessment.
- **A-Level — Senior 5 to Senior 6:** Open to candidates whose UCE results meet the requirements for their chosen subject combination. S6 transfers considered on available space.

### What to Bring
- A completed Grace High School application form
- Photocopy of the PLE results slip (S1) or UCE results (S5)
- Report cards or academic records from the previous school
- Two recent passport-size photographs of the student
- A copy of the student's birth certificate
- A transfer letter from the former school (transfers only)

**Enquire / Apply Now** — submit an enquiry: First Name, Last Name, Phone / WhatsApp, Email (optional), and "Any questions for us?"
